Why It Matters

Three technology areas.
Infinite LinkedIn posts.

⚛️
HALEU

High-Assay Low-Enriched Uranium is the fuel that advanced reactors need and that nobody has yet produced at commercial scale outside of Russia, a supply chain concern the industry addresses primarily through conference panels and strongly-worded policy briefs. We are funding the other approach: startups.

🧱
SiC/SiC Composites

Silicon carbide fiber in a silicon carbide matrix, held together by an interphase that is technically a trade secret at every single company in this space. The material is lighter, harder, more radiation-tolerant, and 40× more expensive than zirconium. The market will definitely be ready by 2027. It has been ready by 2027 since 2014.

🔩
Innovative Structural Materials

"Innovative structural materials" is a DOE funding category broad enough to encompass anything that has ever been near a neutron, which is why it produces the most creative pitch decks in our portfolio. ODS steels, MAX phase ceramics, refractory metal alloys — they all share one property: they need at least one more decade of development.
